About Me

As an experienced architect specializing in workspace interiors and building design, I am passionate about creating functional, aesthetically pleasing, and sustainable environments. With a strong foundation in architectural principles and a keen eye for detail, I focus on crafting spaces that enhance productivity, efficiency, and well-being. My expertise extends to incorporating innovative design solutions, integrating MEP systems seamlessly, and optimizing layouts to meet client needs while maintaining a strong design identity.

Throughout my career, I have worked on diverse projects, ranging from corporate office spaces to commercial establishments, always ensuring a balance between functionality and elegance. I am well-versed in BIM technology, allowing for precise planning, coordination, and execution of designs. My approach emphasizes sustainability, leveraging eco-friendly materials and energy-efficient strategies to create environmentally responsible buildings that align with modern architectural trends.

Beyond design, I am deeply involved in project management, overseeing every stage from conceptualization to execution. Collaboration is at the heart of my work, as I believe in engaging with clients, engineers, and contractors to deliver projects that exceed expectations. With a commitment to continuous learning and innovation, I strive to push the boundaries of design while ensuring practicality and durability in every project I undertake.
